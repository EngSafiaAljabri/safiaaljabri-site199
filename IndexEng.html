About Me

A brief story of my career path and values

Safiya Al-Jabri is a Software Engineer with a self-made journey in the fields of technology and entrepreneurship. I enjoy transforming ideas into real digital products and providing technical consultations that help individuals and organizations grow with confidence and sustainability.

I am a certified trainer by the Ministry of Higher Education, Research and Innovation, and I have delivered workshops in programming and tech entrepreneurship in collaboration with multiple training centers.

I believe that knowledge is only complete when shared, and that empowering people with skills is the true investment in the future of technology.

I am currently seeking my next career opportunity within a work environment that values creativity and combines technical development with meaningful impact — allowing me to continue my passion for building software solutions and creating real value.
